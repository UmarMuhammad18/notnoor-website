Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions.

What happens if I send my own fabric? +
If you send your own fabric, we will discuss the best design that suits the material. The cost will only cover tailoring and additional details (like feathers or stones).
Do you accept returns or exchanges? +
Because all our pieces are made-to-order and custom-fitted, we do not accept returns. However, if there is a fitting issue on our end, we will alter it for free.
How long does custom delivery take? +
Standard production time is 7-14 business days. Delivery time depends on your country (typically 3-7 days for Nigeria, and 7-14 days internationally).
How do I track my international order? +
Once your order is shipped, we will send you a tracking number via WhatsApp/Email so you can monitor your package.

Full Order Guide

How to Order.

Quality, perfection, and timely delivery. Slots are very limited — once all slots are filled, bookings will be closed. Please read the full process below before placing your order.

01
Select Your Product
Browse our collection on Instagram (@not.noor) or on this website. Pick your favourite piece, or contact us to request a custom design or bring your own fabric vision.
02
Send a DM or WhatsApp
Message us via Instagram DM or WhatsApp with your selected piece and any customisation requests. Include reference photos if you have them.
03
Confirm Your Size
Share your measurements with us or use our sizing guide. You may also send in your own fabrics — advise us early so we can plan your timeline.
04
Choose Delivery Method
We deliver worldwide 📦. Let us know your country and we'll provide a shipping quote. See our delivery page for estimated timelines.
05
Make a Payment
Payments should be made on time to secure your slot. Once payment is received, you'll get a confirmation message with your order details.
06
Receive Your Order 🎁
Your beautifully packaged N0tN0Or piece will be dispatched and tracked. We'll keep you updated throughout the process.

How to Measure

Measure over light clothing. Keep the tape measure snug but not tight. Measure bust at the fullest point, waist at the narrowest, and hips at the fullest.

✉️
Send Your Measurements

Not sure about sizing? Send us your exact measurements via WhatsApp or Instagram DM and we'll recommend the best size or create a custom fit.

🧵
Custom Sizing Available

All our pieces can be made to your exact measurements at no extra charge. Simply select "Custom" when ordering and share your measurements.

👗
Length Adjustments

Standard length fits 5'4"–5'7". If you're taller or shorter, let us know your height when ordering so we can adjust accordingly.
